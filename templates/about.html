About StartupOracle

The AI Oracle
for Founders

StartupOracle uses machine learning trained on thousands of real-world startup outcomes to give you a statistically grounded probability of success — instantly.

🧠

How the Model Works

Our ML pipeline is trained on historical startup data from Crunchbase and CB Insights. It learns patterns from funding amounts, round frequencies, and company age to predict success probability with calibrated confidence scores.

Disclaimer

StartupOracle is an educational and analytical tool. Predictions are probabilistic estimates based on historical patterns and should not be treated as financial advice. Startup success depends on countless factors beyond the scope of any model — team, market timing, execution, and luck all play critical roles. Use this tool as one signal among many.
